# 🎙️ Hackathon Pitch Script: Context-Aware Threat Detection

**Time: 2 Minutes**
**Goal: convince judges this is innovative and practical.**

---

**0:00 - 0:20 | The Problem (The Hook)**
"Good morning judges.
In modern cybersecurity, the biggest problem isn't detecting attacks—it's detecting *too many* of them.
Security analysts are drowning in thousands of alerts every day. A failed login at 2 PM is noise. But that same failed login at 3 AM by an administrator? That’s a threat.
Traditional systems treat these events the same. We don't."

**0:20 - 0:50 | The Solution (What we built)**
"We built a **Context-Aware Cyber Threat Detection System**.
Instead of just looking at raw logs, our system understands *context*.
It analyzes three critical dimensions:
1.  **Time**: Is this activity normal for this hour?
2.  **Role**: Should this user be doing this?
3.  **Resource**: How sensitive is the data being accessed?
We combined an **Isolation Forest ML model** with **Rule-Based Heuristics** to not just flag threats, but to *score* them."

**0:50 - 1:30 | The Demo (Show the Dashboard)**
*(Show Dashboard)*
"Here is our live prototype.
As you can see, we ingest raw security logs and instantly visualize the risk.
Look at this event here—flagged as **High Risk (Score: 92)**.
Why?
Our system tells you in plain English: 'Admin login from new IP at 3 AM'.
This isn't a black box. We provide **Explainable AI** so analysts trust the system."

**1:30 - 1:50 | Impact & Future**
"By prioritizing threats with a risk score, we reduce alert fatigue by over 70%.
This simple, effective approach helps teams focus on the fires that actually matter, not just smoke."

**1:50 - 2:00 | Closing**
"Context is King. And our system gives security teams the context they need to win the fight. Thank you."
